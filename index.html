About Me

My career experiences so far have tended to lean towards digital marketing and content creation. After graduating from university in the Summer of 2024 I have taken my learning in a new direction, that of software development. I look forward to the challenge of building up the craft of coding and to the possibilities it may offer after completing the course.

I have a love of literature and film and will continue to hone my creative skills by writing. I am a keen photographer, recently turning to film photography in order to find that wonderful feeling of nostalgia.

If I can blend both the technical and the creative into a profession one day, I will be very content!
