Phoebe Whiteley

Multimedia Creative

Hello! I'm Phoebe, a Gen Z multimedia creator dedicated to expressing myself through my craft, whether that be through film, word or within the digital sphere. I believe story should be at the heart of any project, so for me that must come first. Eager to collaborate and grow, I'm on the lookout for opportunities to innovate and excel.

About Me

My career experiences so far have tended to lean towards digital marketing and content creation. After graduating from university with a BA in English Literature in the Summer of 2024 I have taken my learning in a new direction, that of software development. I look forward to the challenge of building up the craft of coding and to the possibilities it may offer after completing the course.

I have a love of literature and film and will continue to hone my creative skills by writing. I am a keen photographer, recently turning to film photography in order to find that wonderful feeling of nostalgia.

If I can blend both the technical and the creative into a profession one day, I will be very content!
