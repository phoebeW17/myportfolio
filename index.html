About Me

My career experiences so far have tended to lean towards digital marketing and content creation. After graduating from university with a BA in English Literature in the Summer of 2024 I have taken my learning in a new direction, that of software development. After having completed Code Institute’s Full Stack Development Bootcamp I look to further my knowledge in Front-end Development and I now offer my services as a freelance website developer through Fiverr, UpWork and Bark.

I have been a freelance location marshal since April 2024 after completing Liverpool Locations’ Location Assistant course. I am passionate about the Locations department both from a logistical and creative point of view. I have worked on both Film & TV projects in urban and rural locations. For further details please find my CV here.

I have a love of literature and film and will continue to hone my creative skills by writing. I am a keen photographer, recently turning to film photography in order to find that wonderful feeling of nostalgia.
